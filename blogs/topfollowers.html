These Are The 15 Most Followed Indians On Instagram


Instagram is one of the most popular social networking apps in the market right now. Unlike Facebook, the crowd on Instagram is still young, and thus, is preferred more by most users. Instagram offers users a platoform that is versatile but still efficient and clean. Moreover, Instagram is constantly rolling out new features, which keeps things interesting. With the advent of Reels that have a much wider (and far more organic) reach, things have never looked better for content creators on the platform. With so many people flocking to Instagram for entertainment, it is not surprising that many Indian personalities have millions of followers on it. With that, we bring you the 15 most followed Indians on Instagram.

Now, let’s get back to our list of the most followed Indians on Instagram. (As of Jan 2022)
15. Kriti Sanon – 45.2 million followers
Kriti Sanon is a prominent actress who works in the Hindi film industry. She has appeared in multiple successful movies like Luka Chuppi, Heropanti and Bareilly ki Barfi.
14. Disha Patani – 48.2 million followers
Disha Patani is an actress primarily in the Hindi film industry and a model. She has appeared in various successful movies such as M.S. Dhoni: The Untold Story, Baaghi 2 and Bharat.
13. Salman Khan – 48.6 million followers
Salman Khan is one of the most popular actors of Hindi cinema of all time. He’s appeared in over a hundred movies and hosts popular reality TV show Bigg Boss.
12. Sunny Leone – 49.7 million followers
Previously an adult film star, Sunny Leone currently is a model and an actress in the Hindi film industry. She has appeared in films like Ragini MMS 2, Ek Paheli Leela, and Mastizaade.
11. Anushka Sharma – 56.1 million followers
Anushka Sharma is an actress in the Hindi film industry, and she has recently begun producing films alongside her brother. She is married to cricketer Virat Kohli. She has appeared in several successful movies such as Rab Ne Bana Di Jodi, Band Baaja Baraat, Ae Dil Hai Mushkil and PK.
10. Jacqueline Fernandez – 57.5 million followers
Jacqueline Fernandes is a Sri Lankan actress who lives in India and works in the Hindi film industry. She has appeared in several movies such as Murder 2 and Kick.
9. Alia Bhatt – 57.9 million followers
Alia Bhatt is one of the most prominent actresses in the Hindi film industry. She has appeared in several superhit movies, including Raazi, 2 States, Gully Boy, and Highway.
8. Akshay Kumar – 58.4 million followers
kshay Kumar is one of the leading actors to be working in the Hindi film industry. As of 2020, he was the highest paid actor in the industry. He has appeared in over a hundred and forty movies, including superhits like Welcome, Aitraaz, Mr and Mrs Khiladi, Namastey London and Housefull.
7. Katrina Kaif – 60.6 million followers
Katrina Kaif is one of the leading actresses in the Hindi film industry. She has recently launched her line of beauty products as well. She has appeared in several successful movies such as Ek Tha Tiger, Namastey London, Ajab Prem Ki Ghazab Kahani, Zindagi Na Milegi Dobara and Welcome.
6. Deepika Padukone – 63.6 million followers
Deepika Padukone is an actress who predominantly works in the Hindi film industry. She has appeared in several super hit movies, including Piku, Bajirao Mastani, Padmaavat and Yeh Jawani Hai Deewani.
5. Narendra Modi – 64.7 million followers
Narendra Damodardas Modi is an Indian politician and the current Prime Minister of India. He was the Chief Minister of Gujarat for thirteen consecutive years before being elected as the prime minister of India.
4. Neha Kakkar – 67 million followers
Neha Kakkar is a singer and media personality. She is a judge on popular singing reality TV show, Indian Idol.
3. Shraddha Kapoor – 68.6 million followers
Shraddha Kapoor is an Indian actress who works in the Hindi film industry. She has appeared in several movies such as Aashiqui 2, Baaghi, and Ek Villain.
2. Priyanka Chopra – 72.6 million followers
Priyanka Chopra is an actress who works in Indian as well as Hollywood movies. She also appeared in the American thriller series, Quantico. She is married to singer Nick Jonas. Priyanka has appeared in numerous successful movies, such as Bajirao Mastani, Dostana, The White Tiger, and Fashion.
1. Virat Kohli – 178 Million followers
Virat Kohli is an Indian cricketer and the current Captain of the Indian National Cricket team. He is considered to be one of the best batsmen in the world.
